Language of America

The main language used in America is one that many people may not have heard of before. This language is extremely rare, and you are considered lucky if you ever read it or if someone ever speaks it to you. It is known as English.

Here are some examples of what English does and doesn't look like:

English

The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy dog.

Not English

फुर्तीली भूरी लोमड़ी आलसी कुत्ते के उपर से कूद गई।.

Grumpy wizards make toxic brew for the evil Queen and Jack.
