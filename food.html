Food of America

Food comes in all shapes and sizes, but most of it is just big and greasy in America. Americans take pride in their food, often consuming way more fat, sugar and salt than they should. Of course, this has no impact on their health in any way whatsoever because... well it just doesn't. In fact, the obesity rate for Americans is the lowest in the world!
